Build-Ups & Connects

Rebuilt in the actual COQ format: layered "Direct Orals" build-up clues (buzz early for more points) and "Connect" questions where you find the hidden link across unrelated-looking clues. Plus a Clue-do bonus round.

BUILD-UP starts with the most obscure clue (worth the most) and gets easier — exactly like COQ's Direct Orals round. Real quizzing skill is buzzing on clue 1, not waiting for the giveaway.

CONNECT gives you several items that look unrelated. Find what actually links them — COQ runs whole rounds of these, often with pictures instead of text.

CLUE-DO (bonus, bottom of page) is COQ's cryptic wordplay round — one compressed riddle, one answer, no build-up.
